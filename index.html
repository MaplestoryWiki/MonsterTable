It can summon Chaos Mini Beans, and sports a resistance to Physical, Holy, Fire, and Ice. This boss has the same attacks as regular Pink Bean, and some new skills. The new skills include : Seduce (separate from the attack), Reverse Key (separate from the attack), Potential Cancel, Teleporting, and it can transform players into Pink Bean, making them unable to use skills. Chaos Pink Bean has 14 forms throughout the battle, immediately reviving upon death. The last form, the only one to give experience, drops Timeless and Loveless Weapons, Chaos Pink Bean Hat and Suit, Black Bean Hat and Suit, Chaos Pink Bean Mark, and various other items.
Magnus
19012000000000010000012022000000The traitor to the Nova people, and a minion of Darmoor and the Black Mage, is at his full power. He most notably deals more damage than normal mode Magnus. The battle against him can be tough, due to his abilities and surroundings. The map he resides in has a 5 second potion cooldown. Every now and then, green poisonous gas gets sprayed in certain spots, preventing movement or the use of potions for around 5-10 seconds. There are also Meteors that fall to the ground, green ones taking away 50% of your HP, and the blue and purple meteors killing you in one hit. As for Magnus's skills, he can Slow, with the added effect of restricting you from using movement skills such as flash jump and teleport. He can also Zombify. One attack includes him rushing at you, knocking you back with him. He can also spin around, knocking you back. He has 3 attacks that can 1hko, he can summon green shadows of himself that can descend onto certain spots, he can leap into the air with the Kaiserium and inflame it, dropping directly down, and he can leap into the air and prepare a blast of pink flames that fire diagonally downwards on both sides, forcing you to stay either under him or farther away. When he dies, he burns up.
